### Step Flow:

1. Compute **Expected Risk**
2. Compare with **Observed Data**
3. Calculate **Bias Factor**
4. Generate **Corrected Risk**
5. Predict **Future Hotspots**

---

## 📐 Mathematical Definition (Your Core Innovation)

R_e = \alpha T + \beta W + \gamma P + \delta I

Where:

* (R_e): Expected risk

[
B = \frac{R_e}{R_o + \epsilon}
